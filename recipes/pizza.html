Pizza

Description

Everyone loves pizza! Use high-quality sauce, premade dough, and shredded cheese for an easy weeknight meal.

Ingredients

Makes 1 10-12 inch pizza

  • 1 package pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1/2 - 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 6 oz sliced pepperoni
  • flour for dusting surface

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
  2. Lightly cover a flat surface with flour.
  3. Flatten dough with hands and push edges out to make a 10-12 inch circle.
  4. Spread sauce over dough with the back of a spoon, leaving a 1 inch border.
  5. Sprinkle cheese evenly over pizza.
  6. Place pepperoni slices evenly over pizza.
  7. Bake pizza on a baking sheet or pizza stone until crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbling, 10-12 minutes.
  8. Let rest for 3 minutes, then slice and serve.
