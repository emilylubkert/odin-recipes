Shrimp Tacos

Description

It's Taco Tuesday...or whatever day it is! Enjoy these delicious, healthy tacos.

Ingredients

Makes 8 tacos

  • 8 medium tortillas
  • 1 lb medium shrimp
  • 1 Tbsp canola oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • juice from 1 lime
  • for toppings: sliced red cabbage, avocado, sour cream, shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Toss shrimp with oil, cumin, chili powder, salt, and red pepper flakes.
  3. Spread shrimp in a single layer on a baking sheet, and bake for 6 minutes, flipping halfway.
  4. Wrap tortillas in foil and warm in oven for 5 minutes.
  5. Toss baked shrimp with lime juice.
  6. Put 2-3 shrimp in each taco, fill with your desired toppings, and fold in half.
