Grilled Cheese

Description

The secret to a perfectly crispy, but not burned, grilled cheese? Mayonnaise! In this recipe, you'll use mayo to make the best grilled cheese ever.

Ingredients

Makes 1 sandwich

  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese (any type; American is best!)

Steps

  1. Spread mayonnaise evenly on both slices of bread.
  2. Place cheese in between bread so that mayonnaise is on the outside.
  3. Place sandwich on a pan on the stove, cover, and turn on to medium heat.
  4. Check sandwich after 3 minutes; if needed, heat for 1-2 minutes more. Bottom should be golden brown.
  5. Flip sandwich and toast other side for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Remove from heat and let rest 3 minutes before slicing.
  7. Slice in half and enjoy!
